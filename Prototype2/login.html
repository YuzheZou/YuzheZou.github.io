Term of Use
The "COVID Radar" application is to provide users services about COVID-19 latest information and other related data. and hospital services as well. The mechanic is to document users' location to inform users about the high-risk area of COVID.
In term of using this application, Users need to allow system to have access to the Bluetooth and to upload locations to the system. No other personal data will be obtained.
Users have the authority to report mis-record of their location information.
